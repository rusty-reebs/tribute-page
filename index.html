Wayne Gretzky

"The Great One"

Timeline of Gretzky's NHL Career

  • 1979 - Began NHL career with the Edmonton Oilers
  • 1981 - Scored 50 goals in 39 games
  • 1982 - Finished the season with 212 points, an NHL record
  • 1984 - Won his first of four Stanley Cups
  • 1988 - Traded to the Los Angeles Kings
  • 1996 - Traded to the St. Louis Blues
  • 1996 - Signed as a free agent with the New York Rangers
  • 1999 - Retired from the NHL with 2857 points in 1487 regular-season games
